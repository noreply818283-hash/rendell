Case Study

Enigmatic Smile India

Transforming Loyalty with Frictionless Card-Linked Rewards

1. Executive Summary

The Challenge: In a crowded digital landscape, Publishers & Brand Apps face a dual challenge: retaining user attention and driving frequent engagement without inflating operational costs. Traditional programs suffer from friction (coupons, QR codes) leading to drop-offs.

The Solution: Enigmatic Smile India solves this via Single.id, a proprietary technology linking payment cards directly to rewards. Major Indian publishers like Shoppers Stop Club Rewards, NDTV Big Bonus, Dhoni App, Sarvatra Rewards - have unlocked a frictionless, merchant-funded rewards ecosystem that turns every payment into a retention opportunity.

2. The Core Offering

Unlike affiliate links or coupon codes, our technology is infrastructure-native. We eliminate the friction of traditional loyalty programs through invisible tracking and instant gratification.
